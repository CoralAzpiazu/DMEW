About me

My name's Coral, but I’m most known online as Kaleydoskope. I’m a Visual Designer, and my biggest passions are art and science. I'm currently working on a number of editorial illustration projects, with my first illustrated book being published this june! My main focus is on character design and visual developement -I specially enjoy experimenting with color and visual storytelling.

Before studying design, I spent several years studying Aerospace Engineering. But then I had the chance to switch degrees, and studying visual design was such a great opportunity, I had to take it!

I have experience working on digital illustration and had the chance to take part in a VR videogame project developed at the U-tad university, as concept artist. While studying, I have also worked as junior graphic designer in marketing and communication, and I recently took part in an exhibition as an audiovisual creator.
